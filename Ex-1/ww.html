WonderWoman

    Wonder Woman is a superhero created by the American psychologist and writer William Moulton Marston, 
    and artist Harry G. Peter. Marston's wife, Elizabeth, and their life partner, Olive Byrne, are credited
    as being his inspiration for the character's appearance. Wikipedia
    Creators: William Moulton Marston, H. G. Peter
    Significant others: Superman, Batman, Steve Trevor
    Parents: Hippolyta, Zeus
    Notable aliases: Diana Prince, MORE
    Fictional universe: DC Universe
    First appearance: All Star Comics #8 (October 1941/January 1942)
