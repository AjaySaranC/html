Aqua Man

Aquaman is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by DC Comics.
Created by Paul Norris and Mort Weisinger, the character debuted in More Fun Comics #73. The character is a pastiche of Namor.
Parents: Atlanna
Creators: Kurt Busiek, Mort Weisinger, Jackson Guice, Paul Norris
Notable aliases: AC, Aquatic Ace, MORE
TV shows: Smallville, Young Justice, Justice League, Aquaman.
Fictional universes: DC Extended Universe, DC Universe
